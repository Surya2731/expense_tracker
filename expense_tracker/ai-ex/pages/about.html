About AI Expense Tracker

The AI Expense Tracker is an intelligent financial management tool that helps users track expenses, analyze spending patterns, and forecast savings. With advanced AI insights, it enables users to set saving goals and provides predictions on how long it will take to reach them. The application offers features such as voice-enabled expense logging, data visualization, and AI-powered financial recommendations.
